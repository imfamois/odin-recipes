Olive Oil Breakfast Wrap

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • egg
  • cheese
  • Milk
  • salt
  • basil
  • black pepper

Preparation

  1. Take a medium sized skillet and heat it over medium flame. Crack egg into a small bowl and add 5-6 spoonfuls of milk. Add black pepper and salt to taste then whisk egg till mixture comes together.

    Pour egg into skillet and cook while breaking up egg continously. Then place into a bowl on the side

  2. Add 2 tbsp of olive oil into pan and let heat up while adding basil and blackpepper to taste into oil. Cut tomatoes into slices and place into oil. Let each side roast. Then place in a plate on the side.

  3. Place tortilla into pan and pour eggs ontop. Put the slices of tomatoes onto the eggs and 1-2 slices of cheese. Fold the other side of the tortilla onto itself and leave it over heat for 2-3 minutes till cheese melts.

  4. Serve and enjoy
